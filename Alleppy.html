Alleppey

Location: On the Malabar Coast, bordering the Arabian Sea.

Alleppey / Alapphuza

Alleppey offers a rare chance to slow down and immerse yourself in nature and local traditions. Whether you're a honeymooner, solo traveler, family explorer, or wellness seeker — this slice of Kerala welcomes you with open arms, slow waters, and the soft rhythm of village life.

Things to Do in Alleppey

  • Explore the Backwaters
  • Vembanad Lake – Kerala’s largest lake
  • Punnamada Lake – site of the iconic Nehru Trophy Boat Race
  • Kuttanad – one of the few places in the world where farming is done below sea level
  • Alleppey Beach – golden sands, a historic pier, and lighthouse views
  • Marari Beach – tranquil and less commercialized, ideal for quiet retreats
  • Ambalappuzha Sri Krishna Temple – famous for its Palpayasam (sweet pudding)

Location & Accessibility

  • State: Kerala, South India
  • Distance from Kochi (Cochin): ~53 km
  • Nearest Airport: Cochin International Airport (COK)
  • Rail Connectivity: Well-connected via Alappuzha Railway Station
  • By Road: Accessible via NH66

Cuisine & Local Flavors

Alleppey is a food lover’s delight. Don’t miss:

  • Karimeen Pollichathu (Pearl Spot Fish in banana leaf)
  • Toddy – a local fermented coconut drink
  • Sadya – a vegetarian feast
